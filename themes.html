Process

You’re 5 steps away from a new website...

Work with me
01

Discovery

To kick things off we start with a discovery session so I can learn more about your business and what you’re trying to achieve with your new website.

02

Wireframing

I’ll create the structure for the whole site in wireframes to establish the flow of the website and how we’ll present your product or service to best achieve your goals.

03

High-fidelity designs

Next, I’ll turn these wireframes into fully polished designs. At this point you’ll be able to see what the finished website will look like.

04

Website development

Once you’re completely happy with the design I’ll build it in Webflow or Wordpress, using the CMS when possible to make it super easy to update.

05

Launch & training

I’ll help you setup a account before transfering you the website. Once it’s transferred I’ll then train you in how to use the site so you can easily manage it yourself.

How long does a website take to build?

A typical project will take 4-8 weeks, and where it falls in that range depends on:
- Size of the website (i.e. how many pages)
- Complexity of design
- Speed of feedback & communication

How much does a website cost?

There’s a lot of factors that go into how much a website ends up costing, but I use standardised pricing so you know you’re always paying a fair price.
‍
Most new websites will fall into the $500-10k range.

All situations are different though, so get in touch and I can give you a  quote.
